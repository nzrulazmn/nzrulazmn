What Islamind Mind offers

Islamic Mind is an EdTech solution that provides Holistic education in academics synergized with Islamic Elements that is targeted for the global Muslim market. The curriculum covers students aged 5-12 with Islamic education content that is approved and endorsed by Islamic associations.

Learning Activities

Islamic Mind is not the sole method of education; it is used as a tool to encourage learning. It is developed for learning routines and activities that can improve learning generally and assist students acquire better academic outcomes. By participating in educational activities, they can increase learning efficiency and memory retention.

Exciting Challenges

Children's resilience is enhanced through facing challenges, which helps them deal with both intellectual and emotional difficulties. Teaching children how to handle failures and disappointments can improve their self-esteem as they overcome obstacles. Accomplishing difficult tasks or achieving academic milestones gives them a sense of satisfaction and confidence in their abilities.

Profile Personalization

Islamic Mind platform provides special profiles that help children feel more connected to their community. By personalizing their avatars, kids become more emotionally invested and engaged. Customizing their profiles also allows them to showcase their individuality and creativity by adjusting the appearance of their avatars to match their preferences.

Medium for Educational

Islamic Mind is easily accessible through gadgets and the internet, allowing learners to access materials at their convenience. It offers a wide range of topics, allowing learners to explore their own interests. They can learn at their own pace, moving on quickly if they understand the material or reviewing as necessary.

Discover our curriculum

Comprehensive Year-long Courses for Every Stage of Learning

Islam for Little Learners

The Islamic Mind Course for children aged 5-7 has a comprehensive curriculum that covers four semesters. The course has been thoroughly created by Islamic scholars and professors with competence in Islamic studies. The entire course focuses on Angel Jibraeel's (AS) well-known Hadith; Islam, Imaan, and Ihsan.

Perfect Tools for Educators

Islamic Mind is designed to help educators (parents and instructors) and students participate in group and one-on-one situations. Movies, quizzes, experiments, timelines, activity pages, and other tools are available through MindPlus.

It is developed on a quick, scalable, and adaptable platform that allows for the retrieval of approved material, validation, animation, and copyrighting. We employ very appealing animated movies to model conversational while effortlessly integrating ISLAMIC CURRICULUM.

Basic daily occurrences to complicated content-related storytelling

The movies range from simple everyday scenarios to more complex content-related stories that reflect what students are learning in the classroom. Idiomatic and cultural nuances, as expressed through the actions of the endearing and funny characters, model social cues and demonstrate how to use ISLAMIC habits in every day contexts.

An education that will last a lifetime

Islamic Mind is meant to lead a kid from the age of five, when they first encounter spirituality, until the age of fifteen, when their faiths blossoms.

As a result, we've designed our curriculum to be holistic: our year-long, age-appropriate courses complement one another and may be completed sequentially by your kid as they develop.
